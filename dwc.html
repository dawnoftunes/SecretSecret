Don't Waste Christmas

This story revolves around the Mumphrey's. A timeless Newfoundland Christmas tale of one family's laughter, love, and other foolishness.

Skit 1 a bit 'a Christmas cheer

(Scene opens…Mudder and Kevin are in the kitchen by the front door.
Fadder is in the living room, wearing boots.)

Mudder: Kevin my son, git in adavit! An’ shut the door…we’re not heatin’ up the harbour!

Kevin: G’won Mudder b’y! Da b’ys are comin now da once…goin out on skidoo looksee! Loves me new skidoo I got fer Christmas, Mudder.

Mudder: I knows ya do my bebe. But dere’s windows in da house! Ya don’t need the side adav’er!

Kevin: But Mudder! What if de comes and I don’t hear ‘em?!

Mudder: Don’t hear ‘em?! The fuckin’ racket dem skidoos makes! Now, git the Jeesus in adavit ’fore I gits ur fadder!

Kevin: Fadder? Sure w’as he gonna do? He’s on da tare since Tibb’s Eve!

Mudder: Go’on b’y.

Kevin: Mudder! Go look at ‘en! Passed out cold junk under the Christmas Tree! (Fadder’s boots move)

Mudder: Oh, ya saw dat did ya? Yer fadder works hard b’y! He deserves a bit a Christmas cheer.

Kevin: Christmas cheer? Is dat what ya calls it? Fadder over bangin on Aunt Maggies’s door
with her froze up panty hose wrapped ‘roun he’s head singin’ ‘any mummers ‘lowed in?’

Er yes’day when he crashed the youngsters Christmas party down in the hall
…tol em all dere wus no such ting as Sanna Claus! Fadder’s frigged b’y!

Mudder: Don’t ya be sa foolish! D’as all hearsay!

Kevin: We’ll I heard it. I saw it. I’m sayin’ it! Fadder’s gone!

Mudder: Don’t you be talkin’ like dat about yer fadder…dere wudden be no Christmas if it wudden fer yer fadder!

(In the meantime, Fadder has come to…and after a small row with the Christmas Tree,
has staggered into the kitchen.)

Fadder (singing): Hark w’as da niise ye got out by da door? Whadaya at young feller?

Kevin (sounding disgusted): Fadder b’y!

Fadder (slurring and staggering towards Mudder): Loves ya Mudder. Merry Christmas!

Mudder: Now’den d’as n’er state we got to put up wit here da’day! Tank Jeesus da b’ys are here. G’won now bebe. Loves ya!

Kevin: Loves ya too Mudder! (Looks towards fadder, shakes his head says ‘Fadder b’y!’ and leaves)

Fadder (with his arm around her): Have a little drink now Mudder. It’s Christmas. (End scene)

Skit 2 Oh Holy Night

(Scene opens: Billy is sitting at the bar. A girl enters, greets the bartender Joey, and orders a beer.

Billy seems happy to see her.)

Billy: Well d’en! Look what da moose dragged in! Da one and only, Kaylee McKenzie, queen a’da cove!

Gracin’ us all wit her college girl presence!

Kaylee: Who’s dat b’y? Little Billy Mumphrey from up da brook.

Is dat a bobber in your pocket Billy ‘er are ya happy to see me?

Hey Joey, ya might wanna check his snap!

Billy: Saucy as ever I sees! You can take the girl ada da bay but you’ll never git the sauce adav’er!

(holds his beer out to clink Kaylee’s)

Merry Christmas Kaylee McKenzie…(winks) I ‘spects is gonna be a time!

Kaylee (cool af winks back): We’ll see Billy!

(Two girls, Sarah and Gina, come running towards Kaylee screaming excitedly. Kaylee runs to meet them.)

Sarah: Tank Jeesus ur home adavit for Christmas. N’er tare I know!!

Kaylee: Never missed ye a’tall sure!!

They all hold hands jumping up and down screaming ‘Merry Christmas Bitches’.

Then Kaylee shushes the others.

Kaylee: So w’as goin on wit Billy?

Gina: Don’t be talkin’! Dropped July White like a bad habit cause you was coming home fer Christmas!

Been sot on dat stool for four days watchin fer ya!

Sarah: And I heard he tol’ Cracked Chris he wants to take you to Da Wharf fer New Years Eve!

(They again begin to jump and scream whilst holding hands.)

(From the stage, the bar owner, Adrian, speaks)

Adrian: Is that Kaylee McKenzie I sees down by da bar?

Joey, heave a Blackhorse at er we sees if we can git a song adav’er!

(The patrons cheer. Kaylee goes onstage and sings O Holy Night.)

Skit 3 Nan Loves Ya

(The scene opens…Nan is sitting on the davenport. The kitchen smells of Christmas jiggs.

She hears people enter…goes to the door. It’s Greg and Amy and the twins Meg and Peg.)

Nan: Oh come in me duckies! Nan’s some happy ur here. Merry Christmas to ye!

Amy and Peter: Merry Christmas!

Nan: Peter my son, Pop is waiting’ since 10 o’clock d’smorning for someone to have a yarn with ‘en! G’won in now!

(Peter goes to the living room…Pop is asleep)

Meg and Peg (jumping all over her): Merry Christmas Nan!!

Nan: Was Sandy Claus good to ye?

Meg (pouting): Frig Sanna Claus Nan!

Nan: Now’den!

Meg: Uncle Harry told us at the hall he’s not even real!

Amy (recounting the hall events): Dere we were givin out the gifts to the youngsters in da hall

when in he staggers, drunker d’en Fadder John at Midnight Mass,

knocks over the Christmas Tree, takes the mic from the little elf he had frightened da det,

and shouts ‘dere’s no such ting as Sanna Claus’…mortified da works of em!

Young Charlie Peters went into culvusions! Still bawlin’ de sais!

Nan: Never ye mind Uncle Harry…we all knows he’s a few cod short of Highliner fish stick.

And ya knows he had a 60 pounder a Lambs in ‘en!

Peg: No Nan b’y it’s true! When he pulled the beard of ‘en it was Jimmy Tangs fadder.

And I knows Jimmy Tang is not an elf,

so Sanna Claus can’t be e’s fadder!

Meg: So frig Uncle Harry…frig Sanna Claus…and (Peg joins in) frig Christmas!

Nan: Well ye wun’t be wantin n’er present then!

Meg and Peg: Presents? Sure we loves Christmas Nan!

(They run to the tree)

(Door opens…enter Aunt Bun, Uncle Harry and Kevin.

Amy (to Uncle Harry): Showin’ yer face are ya? All what you’re after gettin’ on wit?

Uncle Harry (visibly still drunk): Well would ya rather see me arse!

(elludes to pulling his zipper and showing Amy he’s arse. Amy gasps.

He hugs Nan and goes to the living room to join Pop and Peter,

who are now both sleeping…otw whispering to Meg and Peg as they open presents,

’There’s no such ting as Sanna Claus’. They scream ‘frig off Uncle Harry’…Pop and Peter never budges)

Meg ang Peg sing the second bit of Don't Waste Christmas
